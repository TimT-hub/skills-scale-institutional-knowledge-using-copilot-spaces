# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

Welcome! This documentation is the entry point to how OctoAcme plans, delivers, and improves projects. Use it as a quick reference whether you're joining a project for the first time or need to align on process expectations.

## Purpose and scope
These docs explain the OctoAcme project management process end-to-end, from idea intake to retrospective follow-through. They are intended for cross-functional teams, including product, engineering, QA, and project management roles.

## OctoAcme principles
Our project approach is grounded in five principles:
- **Customer-first** outcomes
- **Iterative delivery** in small, testable increments
- **Clear ownership** for decisions, milestones, and communication
- **Data-informed decisions** using measurable outcomes
- **Psychological safety** that supports candid feedback and continuous learning

## Project lifecycle overview
At a high level, OctoAcme work moves through six major process stages, with supporting role guidance:
1. **Initiation** — validate the problem, goals, stakeholders, and success metrics.
2. **Planning** — define scope, backlog, milestones, dependencies, and ownership.
3. **Execution & Tracking** — deliver increments, track progress, and escalate blockers.
4. **Risk & Communication** — manage risks proactively and maintain clear stakeholder updates.
5. **Release & Deployment** — prepare, deploy, verify, and communicate releases safely.
6. **Retrospective & Improvement** — capture learnings and convert them into action.
7. **Roles & Personas** — clarify responsibilities and collaboration expectations across the team.
